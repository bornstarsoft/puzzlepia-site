Ringzzle is a free color rings puzzle game from BornstarSoft. Place colorful rings on the board, clear matching color lines, trigger Color Burst moments, and chase your best score in a browser-playable HTML5 puzzle with no install.

Start on the official Ringzzle site: [Play Ringzzle](https://ringzzle.com/play/).

New to color rings? Read the official [How to Play](https://ringzzle.com/how-to-play/) guide.

Ringzzle also has an [anonymous leaderboard](https://ringzzle.com/leaderboard/) for submitted scores. It is nickname-only and does not require login or email.

You can also visit the [Ringzzle game site](https://ringzzle.com/) for the main game page.
