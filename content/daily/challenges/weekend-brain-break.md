Use this weekend challenge as a light mixed puzzle break.

## Puzzle prompt

Complete three tiny tasks:

1. Find a four-letter puzzle word.
2. Count upward from 1 to 8.
3. Choose one target color and clear only that color.

Keep each task simple and finish one before starting the next.

## Hint

Treat the challenge as three small puzzles, not one large puzzle.

## Answer

A complete run means you found the word, followed the number order, and cleared the target color without changing the goal midway.
