Use this quick word search idea as a warm-up before playing the interactive grid.

## Puzzle prompt

Find these four words in your mind as straight lines: STAR, MOON, MINT, and PLAY.

Start by looking for the first letter of each word. Then check right, down, and diagonally.

## Hint

Short words are easiest when you search for the first and last letter together.

## Answer

The target words are STAR, MOON, MINT, and PLAY.
