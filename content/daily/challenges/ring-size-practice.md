Ring puzzles often ask you to think about both color and size.

## Puzzle prompt

A cell already has a small mint ring and a large pink ring.

Can you place a medium gold ring in the same cell?

Can you place another small ring there?

## Hint

In the Puzzlepia starter rule, a cell can hold different sizes, but it cannot hold the same size twice.

## Answer

You can place the medium gold ring because the medium size is still open. You cannot place another small ring because the cell already has a small ring.
