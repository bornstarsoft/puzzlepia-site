This challenge focuses on one clear visual goal.

## Puzzle prompt

Imagine a 5 by 5 grid with pink, mint, gold, and purple tiles.

Your task is to clear only the pink tiles. Ignore the other colors unless a rule tells you otherwise.

## Hint

Scan row by row so you do not miss a pink tile near the edge.

## Answer

The puzzle is complete when every pink tile has been selected and no pink targets remain.
