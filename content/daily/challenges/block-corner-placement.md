Corners can help organize a block puzzle board, but only if they keep space open.

## Puzzle prompt

Imagine a 5x5 board with an empty top-left corner. You have an L-shaped piece.

Choose the better plan:

- Place the L-shape tightly into the corner and keep the center open.
- Place the L-shape in the middle and leave tiny gaps near the edge.

## Hint

Good corner moves usually make the board cleaner, not more broken.

## Answer

The better plan is to place the L-shape tightly into the corner while keeping the center open. This preserves more flexible space for future pieces.
