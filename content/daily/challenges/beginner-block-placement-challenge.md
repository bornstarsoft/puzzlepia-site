This challenge is about thinking before placing, not speed.

## Puzzle prompt

Imagine a small 6 by 6 board. You have three pieces: a 3-block line, a 2 by 2 square, and a small corner piece.

Choose placements that keep one large open area on the board.

## Hint

Place the largest or least flexible piece before the board becomes crowded.

## Answer

A safe approach is to place the line along an edge, place the square near a corner, and keep the center open for the corner piece.
