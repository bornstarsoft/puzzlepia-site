This prompt is a tiny color scanning warmup.

## Puzzle prompt

Look at this row:

`Mint  Pink  Mint  Gold  Mint`

How many mint cells are there?

## Hint

Scan once from left to right, then count only the cells labeled `Mint`.

## Answer

There are three mint cells.
