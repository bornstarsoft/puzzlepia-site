Start with one short word before opening a full puzzle.

## Puzzle prompt

Find the word `STAR` in this mini row:

`S T A R P M`

Read from left to right and mark the four letters that complete the word.

## Hint

Start with `S`, then check whether the next letters spell the word in order.

## Answer

The answer is `S T A R`, using the first four letters of the row.
