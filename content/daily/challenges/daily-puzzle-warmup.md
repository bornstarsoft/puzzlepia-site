This warmup helps you choose a puzzle mood before playing.

## Puzzle prompt

Pick one goal for today's short puzzle break:

- Find hidden words.
- Place pieces cleanly.
- Match colors.
- Follow numbers in order.

Choose the goal that feels easiest to start.

## Hint

The best daily puzzle is often the one you can begin right away.

## Answer

There is no single fixed answer. The challenge is complete when you choose one puzzle goal and play a short session with it.
