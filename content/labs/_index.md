Puzzlepia Labs is a quiet space for testing small web puzzle ideas before they become part of the main Daily Puzzle collection.

The current Puzzlepia site remains focused on stable [Daily Puzzles](/daily/) and BornstarSoft [game pages](/games/). Lab pages may preview future directions, but they should stay lightweight, honest, and easy to remove or improve.

## Current lab placeholders

- [Phaser Word Search](/labs/phaser-word-search/) - a future test page for a possible Phaser-powered Word Search prototype.
